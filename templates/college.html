About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was started in the academic year 2008-2009. The department has highly qualified and dynamic faculty members specialized in advanced areas. The department has good infrastructure and it imparts high quality education  structured to meet the present day needs. The CSE Students Association XENIOZ organizes activities like Paper Presentation, Project Display and Technical Quiz.

 

To provide an academically conductive environment for students to reach their full potential in computer science and engineering, thereby inculcating an affinity for lifelong educational renewal and preparing them to face the challenges of the modern high tech society with human values and ethics.
